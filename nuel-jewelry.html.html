About Nuel

Designer-owned and operated in Seattle

Nuel Jewelry Design and Curation was born from a childhood love of rocks and minerals that developed into a passion for gemology combined with an artistic desire to create. This venture developed organically out of a hobby and artistic outlet.

Based in Seattle, our independent studio specializes in pieces that blend contemporary sophistication with vintage-inspired details and occasional playful flourishes. We believe that luxury shouldn't come at the expense of ethics, which is why every gemstone and precious metal reflects our commitment to responsible sourcing and sustainable practices.

Whether you're drawn to the clean lines of modern design, the ornate beauty of art nouveau, or the unexpected charm of whimsical accents, you'll find something that speaks to your individual style in our collection.
